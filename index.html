Bridging Portland's Digital Divide Together

Portland Digital Bridge Network is a volunteer-led mutual aid network dedicated to bridging the digital divide in Portland, Oregon. We’re not a nonprofit or for-profit business — we’re a community of neighbors providing free tech support, tech classes, and aid for those needing technology, all without profit motives, hierarchy, or bureaucracy.

Our Key Services

Free Tech Support

In need of tech support or repairs but don't have the funds? We can help with free tech support and hardware repairs to help our neighbors in need and to reduce e-waste.

Free Tech Classes

Interested in learning about tech? Interested in a possible career in tech? Need to learn something for a new job? We offer free classes on a range of topics from the very basics of tech to more in-depth topics.

Aid Fund

Need a new computer but can't afford one? Need a new phone? Need help with your internet or phone bill? We help with getting people the access they need to essential technology in today's rapidly increasing digital world.
