Volunteer Opportunities

Are you interested in volunteering? Do you have tech skills and want to share those with the community? Do you have other skills that aren't necessarily tech skills that can still help? If so, we'd be happy to have you. There are no time commitments for volunteering. Volunteer as little or as much as you would like.

If you are interested in volunteering for the first time, we will invite you to attend our next bi-monthly volunteer get together and training. This is an optional event for our existing volunteers, but we highly encourage everyone who volunteers to attend to meet new volunteers and socialize and get to know our other volunteers better.

To keep our community safe and healthy we require that all volunteers wear masks when attending in-person events. This helps make sure that we are not still contributing to the spread of COVID and other communicable diseases that are harmful and dangerous to our immunocompromised and disabled community members.

Here is an overview of our opportunities:

  • Tech Support

    • Provide free technical assistance to community members, including troubleshooting, repairs, setup, and advice for various devices and software.

  • Teaching

    • Lead or assist with classes covering a range of topics, from basic device usage to more advanced skills like coding or IT support.

  • Community Outreach

    • Build connections with local organizations, promote the network's services, and raise awareness to attract participants and volunteers.

  • Purchasing and Procurement

    • Manage sourcing and purchasing of tech supplies, tools, and other resources needed for classes, tech support, and community donations.

  • Device Refurbishment and Repair

    • Assess and repair donated devices, ensuring they are functional and ready to be distributed to community members in need.

  • Fundraising and Grant Writing

    • Help secure funding by writing grant proposals, organizing fundraisers, or reaching out to local businesses and sponsors.

  • Web Development and Content Management

    • Maintain and update the network's website, upload class schedules and recordings, and manage a potential blog or other digital tools.

  • Event Planning and Logistics

    • Coordinate in-person classes, tech support drop-ins, and other events, ensuring all logistical needs - space, equipment, and volunteers - are met.

  • Translation and Accessibility Services

    • Translate materials into multiple languages and ensure that resources and events are accessible to people with disabilities.

  • Communications and Social Media

    • Manage social media platforms, write newsletters, and create promotional materials to engage with the community and share updates.

  • Inventory and Resource Management

    • Organize and catalog donated devices and supplies, and track distributed resources to ensure effective use of donations.

Partner With Us

What Partner With Us?

  • You will be helping bridge the digital divide here in Portland, Oregon.
  • You will be supporting equitable access to technology and digital literacy.
  • You will be able to enhance your organization's community impact and visibility.

How You Can Partner With Us

  1. Provide Financial Support
    • Sponsor specific initiatives, like tech classes or aid funds.
    • Help cover operational costs, such as class venue rentals or device repairs.
  2. Donate Equipment or Supplies
    • Provide refurbished devices, software licenses, tools, or other tech-related resources
    • Reduce e-waste by channeling surplus technology to those in need
  3. Offer Expertise
    • Collaborate on tech-related events or classes with your staff's expertise
    • Share knowledge and resources to enhance our programs
  4. Collaborate on Outreach
    • Help promote our services to your network
    • Refer individuals who may benefit from our tech support or classes
  5. Provide Space
    • Allow us to use your facilities for hosting classes, events, or drop-in tech support

Recognition for Our Partners

If you partner with us, we will highlight our partnership in a number of ways.

  • Your logo and name will be featured on our website and event materials
  • Your partnership with us will be highlighted in newsletters and/or social media
  • There may be opportunities for co-branding on events or initiatives

Interested in Becoming a Partner?

If you're a business who would like to partner with us, please give us a call at 555-555-5555, email us at example@example.com, or fill out this form, and we will get back to you as soon as possible. If you are interested in collaborating in other ways, please let us know, and we would be happy to discuss any and all possibilities.
